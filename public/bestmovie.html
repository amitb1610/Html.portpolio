Troy

I have watched this more than 5 times and still this is the best historical film (though it might not be completely faithful to iliad) and I would recommend this to everyone! I wish there was another powerful movie like this for ODDYSSEY

3 Idiots

Three Idiots is a groundbreaking Bollywood hit that was released ahead of its time. This film is a comedy with great acting, unique characters, a complex plot, and, most importantly, a very inspiring film about choosing the correct route in life.
